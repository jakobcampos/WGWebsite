ABOUT US

HOW IT ALL STARTED

Our journey into game development began in March 2023, sparked by a shared passion for video games. As a group of enthusiasts, we refined our skills and navigated the complexities of game creation. This led us to formalize our bond and establish our own independent studio, translating our passion into professional game development.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

At Wildcard Gameworks, our philosophy of game development revolves around the triad of innovation, passion, and community. Innovation is at the heart of our operations; we believe in pushing boundaries, exploring uncharted territories, and crafting experiences that are novel and engaging. We don't see game development as a process of mere content creation, but as a platform to manifest our creativity.
